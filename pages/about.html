Our Story

A Legacy of Precision Since 1992

ChronoLux began as a discreet bench in a Mayfair mews, where founder Alexander Hartley restored pocket watches for local collectors. What started as a one-man operation quickly grew into a full workshop as word spread about his meticulous attention to detail and honest pricing.

Over three decades, we have expanded our services to include fine jewellery repair while never compromising the horological standards that built our reputation. Today, our workshop handles everything from everyday quartz battery changes to complete mechanical overhauls of vintage chronographs.

Every piece that enters our doors is logged, photographed, and tracked through our repair system - so you always know exactly where your treasured item is and when to expect it back.

Lead Craftsman

Meet Our Master Watchmaker

Three decades of horological expertise guiding every repair in our workshop.

Alexander Hartley

Founder & Master Watchmaker - AWCI Certified

Alexander trained at the British School of Watchmaking before founding ChronoLux in 1992. Specialising in mechanical and automatic movements, he has restored timepieces from Patek Philippe, Jaeger-LeCoultre, and countless heritage maisons.

His philosophy is simple: treat every watch as if it were your own. Alexander personally inspects complex commissions before they leave the atelier and continues to mentor the next generation of master craftspeople.

  • AWCI Certified Master Watchmaker
  • 30+ years of horological experience
  • Specialist in vintage mechanical restorations

Behind the Scenes

Our Workshop

Step inside our Mayfair workshop and you will find a space designed for precision. Each bench is equipped with professional-grade tools, loupes, and testing equipment calibrated to manufacturer specifications.

We maintain separate areas for watch movement work and jewellery soldering to prevent cross-contamination of materials. A dedicated clean room handles ultrasonic cleaning and polishing, while our pressure-testing station verifies water resistance after every seal replacement.

Security is paramount - all items are stored in individual locked cabinets overnight, and our premises are monitored around the clock.
