Before You Visit

What to Bring

Help us assess and repair your item efficiently by bringing the following when you drop off.

  • The item itself

    Bring the watch or piece of jewellery that needs repair, including any removable parts.

  • Straps, chains, or spare links

    Include original straps, bracelets, chains, or extra links if relevant to the repair.

  • Warranty or service papers

    Original documentation helps us verify authenticity and service history where applicable.

  • Original box or pouch

    If available, bring the original packaging for safe storage while your item is with us.

  • Photo ID

    A valid photo ID is required when dropping off high-value items for security purposes.
